Saver Protocol is a revolutionary "No-Loss" GameFi platform that combines the power of DeFi (Decentralized Finance) with the entertainment of Gaming.

Inspired by Alchemix's "Self-Repaying Loans" mechanism, Saver Protocol allows users to play games, bet, and earn without ever fearing the loss of their initial principal.

ğŸŒŸ The Core Concept

Saver Protocol operates on the principle of "Play with Yield - Keep the Principal":

Deposit: Users deposit Stablecoins (USDT, USDC) into the Saver Vault.

Yield Generation: The Vault automatically deploys funds to farm yield on reputable DeFi protocols (Aave, Compound, Yearn...) with a projected APY of ~20%.

Borrow: Users borrow $srUSD (Saver USD) game tokens based on the future yield generated (up to 50% LTV).

Play & Earn: Use $srUSD to participate in PvP games.

If you Win: You receive more $srUSD and can swap it for real money.

If you Lose: You only lose the borrowed $srUSD. This debt is automatically repaid over time by the yield generated from your deposited principal. Your principal remains safe.
